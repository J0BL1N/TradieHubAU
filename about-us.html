How it works

Simple flow, clear job stages, and a completion process that protects both sides.

1) Post

Customers post a job with trade, suburb, and a clear description.

2) Apply

Tradies apply with availability, experience, and a message.

3) Agree

Once accepted, the job moves through agreed and in-progress stages.

4) Complete

Completion requires photos plus confirmation from both sides.

Pricing (tier-based fees)

No subscriptions. No paying for leads that go nowhere. Pricing scales fairly with job value and is capped for big installs.
